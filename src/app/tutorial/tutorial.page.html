Welcome to MAINT247

The Maint247 app is a practical, modern and simple manager propierty for landlords and tenants. Your main functions is manage your properties, manage maintenance of your units and rental receipt management.

Manager your properties

Maint247 app lts you know everything in real time, everything that is happening on your property, a dashboard for you to manage your property..

Manage Maintenance

Maint247 app manages the maintenance of your units, the tenant can open an order, take photos and send to the application and and monitor the maintenance.

Rental Receipt Management

Maint247 app the tenant can pay the rent online and directly on the mobile, By transferring money directly to the owner's account via PayPal, there is no more delay.
